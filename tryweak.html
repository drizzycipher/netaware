📘 Story

You’ve just joined a popular online community where people share ideas, photos, and stories. As you create your account, you’re asked to set a password, something quick and easy. But what you don’t realize is that hackers are constantly scanning accounts for weak passwords to break into. In this simulation, you’ll experience how easily an attacker can exploit simple passwords, and learn how small changes can make your digital identity much safer.
